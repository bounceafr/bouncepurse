## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Go to the Bounce Purse website.
2. Click **Register** and fill in your name, email, and a password.
3. Check your email inbox for a **verification link** — click it to activate your account.
4. Once verified, you will be asked to complete your **player profile** (country selection). Do this before you can access the rest of the platform.

### Logging In

1. Visit the site and enter your email and password.
2. If you have enabled **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**, you will also be asked for a one-time code from your authenticator app.

## Your Dashboard

After logging in, your **Dashboard** is the first page you see. It gives you a quick overview of everything happening on the platform.

### What You'll Find on the Dashboard

| Section | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Visitor Stats** | How many people have visited the platform over the past 90 days |
| **Recent Games** | A live feed of recently submitted or approved games |
| **Games Trend** | A chart showing games submitted over the past 7 days and 6 months |
| **Your Rankings** | Your current rank and score in each game format you play |
| **Pathway Status** | Whether you are currently eligible for the Pathway Programme |

## Submitting a Game

To record a game you played:

1. Go to **My Games** or use the **Submit Game** button (visible to players).
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Title** — a short descriptive name for the game
   - **Court** — where the game was played (select from the registered courts)
   - **Format** — e.g. Singles or Doubles
   - **Date Played** — the date the game took place
   - **Result** — Win, Loss, or Draw
   - **Points** — the points value associated with this game
3. Click **Save**.
4. (Optional) After saving, you can **upload a video** of the game via the upload page. Videos are stored securely on Vimeo.

> Your game will be marked **Pending** until a moderator reviews and approves it.

---

## Game Status — What Does It Mean?

Every game has one of three statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | The game has been submitted and is waiting for a moderator to review it |
| **Approved** | A moderator confirmed the game is valid. It now counts toward your ranking and generates a financial allocation |
| **Flagged** | The game was reviewed but could not be approved — usually due to missing information, a video issue, or a conduct concern. You may be asked to provide more details |

> Only **Approved** games affect your rank and create a financial allocation.

## Leaderboard

The **Leaderboard** page shows a ranked list of all players on the platform.

- Players are ranked by their **score**, which is calculated from their wins, losses, total games, and how recently they have been playing.
- The leaderboard is broken down by **game format** (e.g. Singles, Doubles).
- Rankings are automatically updated every time a game is approved.

**How is my score calculated?**

Your score is a weighted combination of:
- Number of **wins**
- Number of **losses**
- **Total games** played
- **Recent activity** (games played in the last 30 days)

The exact weights are configured by the administrators and may change over time.

## Ledger (Your Allocations)

The **Ledger** page shows a breakdown of the financial allocations generated by your approved games.

### What is an Allocation?

Every time one of your games is **approved**, the points value of that game is automatically split into four financial buckets:

| Bucket | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Insurance** | Covers player insurance contributions |
| **Savings** | Goes into your personal savings pool |
| **Pathway** | Supports the Pathway Programme for elite players |
| **Administration** | Covers platform and administrative costs |

The percentages for each bucket are set by the administrators.

### Reading Your Ledger

The Ledger shows:
- A list of all your approved games with the amounts allocated per bucket
- A **summary** at the top showing your running totals for each category

## Your Profile & Settings

Access your account settings from the **Settings** menu (usually in the top-right corner).

### Profile Settings

- **Name & Email** — Update your display name and email address.
- **Player Profile** — Set your country/region. This is required to use the platform.

- You can change your password at any time from **Settings → Password**.
- Enter your current password and then choose a new one.

### Appearance

- Switch between **Light Mode** and **Dark Mode** from **Settings → Appearance**.
- Your preference is saved automatically.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account. When enabled, you will need both your password and a code from an authenticator app to log in.

### Setting Up 2FA

1. Go to **Settings → Two-Factor Authentication**.
2. Click **Enable**.
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (e.g. Google Authenticator, Authy).
4. Enter the 6-digit code shown in your app to confirm setup.
5. Save your **recovery codes** somewhere safe — you will need these if you ever lose access to your authenticator app.

### Disabling 2FA

1. Go to **Settings → Two-Factor Authentication**.
2. Click **Disable** and confirm with your password.

## The Pathway Programme

The **Pathway Programme** recognises top-performing, well-behaved players and opens special opportunities for them.

### How Do I Become Eligible?

To be marked as a **Pathway Candidate**, you must meet all three of these criteria (the exact numbers are set by administrators and may be updated):

| Requirement | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Minimum Approved Games** | You must have had a certain number of games approved |
| **Rank Requirement** | Your current rank must be within a top-N threshold |
| **Conduct** | You must have fewer than a set number of flagged games |

### How Do I Check My Status?

Your Pathway eligibility is displayed on your **Dashboard** in the Pathway Status card. It will show either:

- **Eligible** — You meet all criteria and are a Pathway Candidate
- **Not Yet Eligible** — You haven't met one or more criteria yet (keep playing and maintaining good conduct!)

Eligibility is recalculated automatically every time a game is approved or the criteria are updated.

## Tips & Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: My game has been Pending for a long time. What should I do?**
A: Moderation times can vary. Make sure your video upload (if required) has completed successfully. If your game has been pending for an unusually long time, contact an administrator.

---

**Q: My ranking hasn't updated after a game was approved.**
A: Rankings recalculate automatically in the background. It may take a few moments. Try refreshing the Leaderboard page.

---

**Q: Can I edit a game after submitting it?**
A: Games can be edited by administrators. If you made an error, contact an admin with your correction.

---

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to **Settings → Profile** and scroll to the bottom. There is a **Delete Account** option. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

---

**Q: I lost access to my 2FA app. How do I log in?**
A: Use one of the **recovery codes** you saved when setting up 2FA. If you no longer have those, contact an administrator to help recover your account.

---

**Q: What devices are supported?**
A: Bounce Purse is a web application that works on any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on desktop, tablet, or mobile.
